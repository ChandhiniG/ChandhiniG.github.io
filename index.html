A little about me

An inquisite learner and always hopeful

My name is Chandhini Grandhi. I am a graduate student in Computer Engineering at University of California, San Diego. I am from India and I have a Bachelors degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering from Madras Institute of Technology, Anna University. I worked at Rotork Controls as Software Engineer in the core Research and Development Team. At Rotork, I led and maintained the Embedded software for a product that communicates over CAN bus. At UCSD, I am majoring in Machine learning. This is where the intersection of Machine Learning and Systems fascinates me.

Below are a few of my skills:

  • Languages (more than 5000 lines): C, C++, Python
  • Tools: Jupyter notebooks, Visual Studio, Git, Mercurial, Gerrit, JIRA
  • Frameworks: Pandas, Numpy, Scikit-learn, TensorFlow, Matplotlib, Pytorch
